# My Cranbi Homepage Brief

## Overall Direction

The website should follow:

- `Kama Ayurveda` for overall structure, hierarchy, and premium Ayurvedic brand mood
- `Caudalie` for typography mood, white background styling, spacing, and soft minimal luxury
- `SoulTree` for ingredient storytelling and rooted Ayurvedic language
- `SkinCeuticals` and `Caudalie` for quiz, routine-building, loyalty, and conversion-focused sections
- `Forest Essentials` for gifting, influencer/reels placement, and premium beauty merchandising cues

This should feel like:

- soft luxury Ayurvedic
- premium but not overly royal
- fresh and clean, not beige-heavy
- white, airy, and product-led
- elegant and giftable

## Core Visual Rules

- Main structure and section flow should be inspired by `Kama Ayurveda`
- Main page background should be `white`, similar to the current `Caudalie` feel
- Gold should be used as an accent from the packaging, not as the main site background
- The product packaging should provide the richness; the website should provide softness, clarity, and elegance
- The site should not feel too clinical, too youthful, too dark, or too ornate

## Visual System

### Fonts

- Heading font: `Cormorant Garamond`
- Body font: `Manrope`

These should create:

- elegant serif beauty-brand headlines
- clean and highly readable ecommerce body text

### Colors

- Main background: `#FFFFFF`
- Alternate section background: `#FAF7F2`
- Border / soft divider color: `#E9DFD2`
- Gold accent: `#C9974D`
- Orange accent: `#C96A2B`
- Deep brown text: `#4A3427`

### Background Direction

- Mostly white backgrounds
- Light alternate backgrounds only in selected sections
- No heavy cream or beige across the whole site
- No dark luxury styling
- No bright gold gradients
- Use subtle softness and generous whitespace

## Homepage Structure

The homepage should follow this order:

1. Announcement bar
2. Header
3. Hero section
4. Shop by category
5. Bestsellers
6. Build my routine / quiz
7. Hero ingredients
8. Brand story
9. Gift sets
10. Travel minis
11. Ritual video / reels
12. Reviews / social proof
13. Community signup
14. Loyalty / referral / service strip
15. Footer

## Final Reference Hierarchy

This hierarchy should be followed to keep the homepage cohesive:

1. `Kama Ayurveda` for main structure and premium Ayurvedic ecommerce layout
2. `Caudalie` for white background styling, typography mood, spacing, and polished softness
3. `SoulTree` for ingredient storytelling and rooted Ayurvedic content tone
4. `SkinCeuticals` and `Caudalie` for functional conversion features like quiz and routine discovery
5. `Forest Essentials` for gifting, social proof, and premium merchandising cues

## What To Avoid

- overly golden or beige-heavy page backgrounds
- dark luxury styling
- cluttered hero banners
- overly clinical skincare layouts
- overly youthful or colorful beauty branding
- mixing too many reference styles equally

## Simple Guiding Principle

Let the `My Cranbi` product packaging provide the richness.

Let the website provide:

- white-space
- softness
- clarity
- elegance
- premium Ayurvedic credibility
